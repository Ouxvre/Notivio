Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Notivio.

How does the "Virtual Sensor" work?

Our system continuously monitors your connected calendars and candidate status. It acts as a background process that triggers alerts based on pre-defined rules, ensuring no conflict goes unnoticed.

Can I integrate with Google Calendar?

Yes, Notivio supports 2-way sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar out of the box.
